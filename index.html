Our Heritage

Established on 19 August 1930, Jagannath Barooah University represents the academic soul of Upper Assam.

Initially named Upper Assam College and later Jorhat College, it was renamed in 1938 to honor Jagannath Barooah, the first graduate of Upper Assam (1872). The legacy lives on in the commodious 'Borpatra Kutir', generously donated by Murulidhar Barooah.

  • Founder Principal: Prof. Krishna Kanta Handique
  • Accreditation: NAAC 'A' Grade (2003)
  • University Status: Jagannath Barooah University Act 2023
